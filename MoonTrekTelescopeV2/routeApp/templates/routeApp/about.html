The MoonTrek Team

MoonTrek is a collaborative project between Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL) and the Senior Design Team of Cal State LA Students. Together we aim to achieve an application to simplify and bridge the gap between someone's photo of a moon that was taken by themselves or from someone else and display useful information such as Craters, Maria, and Apollo Landing Sites on the moon.

Moontrek Group Observing The Moon

Our project was split into four sections: Telescope Integration, focusing on implementing a live feed from a telescope to Moontrek. 3D Modeling, focusing on creating an interactive 3D model of the Sun, Moon, and Earth based off the uploaded Moon Image. Image Registration, focusing on fine-tuning the algorithms used to detect coordinates on the moon to display a proper image of the moon. User Interface, focusing on creating a seamless and simple to use interface that is both quick and easy to use.
